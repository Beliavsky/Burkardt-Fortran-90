SPHERE_FIBONACCI_GRID
Fibonacci Spiral Grid on a Sphere

SPHERE_FIBONACCI_GRID, a FORTRAN90 code which constructs a grid of points using the Fibonacci spiral over the surface of a sphere in 3D.
